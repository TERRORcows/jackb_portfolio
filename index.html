About Me

Hi, I'm Jack. I'm an environment artist, 3D modeller, and composer. I work for multiple volunteer projects such as Aperture 77, and Welcome To Eltrich. I've been working in the Source Engine since late 2017, and I've been making game-ready 3D models and environments in Blender since 2020. Music has been a hobby of mine for a long time, and I'm currently working on an original OST for Aperture 77, Welcome To Eltrich, and several of my friends projects.
